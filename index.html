Publica

open | intuative | research-driven

Publica is an open source peer review platform which aims to allow researchers, peer reviewers, and jounrals to more efficiently communicate, collaborate, and contribute to the body of knolwedge. Currently in development, the platform will give a space for reasearchers to post their work, academics to review the papers, and academic journals to search and contact contributers. Please sign up for more information and follow our progress on twitter!
