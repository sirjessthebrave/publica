Publicus

Research for the 21st century

Research fulfills our drive to understand more about the universe we live in and our role in it. Through research we can explore the cosmos, unravel the mysteries of genes and disease, and more fully understand our relationships with one another.

Unfortunately, the system for sharing, evaluating, and rewarding research still relies on ineffective relics of the 1700s that limit us in our search for truth. Publicus aims to obliterate those limits.

Publicus is an open platform for scholars to instantly share and immediately get feedback on their work from a community of peers. With Publicus, research will be evaluated based on its own merits, not its affiliations

Publicus is under development. Stay tuned for updates.
