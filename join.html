Publicus

Research for the 21st century

Researchers are under constant pressure to produce sensational results, get into selective journals, and look more appealing to funders. In this ‘publish or perish’ world, some important research never sees the light of day because it isn’t flashy. Other research makes headlines, only to be retracted later because it can’t be reproduced. These are symptoms of bad incentives in research.

Publicus will realign these incentives to allow research to be evaluated based on its own merit by a community of peers. Think tripadvisor for research papers.
