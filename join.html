Publicus

Publicus’ mission is to improve the way research is done.

Publicus is on a mission to improve the way research is done. We will accomplish that mission by providing an open platform for scholars to share and collectively review new work. With Publicus, research will be evaluated based on its own merits, not its affiliations.
